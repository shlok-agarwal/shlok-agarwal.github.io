ingredients:
  - Serves 3 (Makes approx 6 muffins)
  - 2/3 cup rolled oats (dry)
  - 1 medium ripe banana
  - 1/3 cup milk (e.g., unsweetened almond)
  - 1 large egg (or 1 chia egg)
  - 2 tsp coconut oil (melted)
  - 2 tsp coconut sugar
  - 1/4 cup vanilla protein powder
  - 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  - 1/8 tsp baking soda
  - 1/4 tsp baking powder
  - Toppings: 3 tbsp peanut butter, 3 medium bananas

steps:
  - Preheat oven to 400F (200C).
  - Whisk the egg in a mixing bowl (or prepare chia egg).
  - Blend rolled oats to make flour.
  - Mash the banana.
  - Add oat flour, mashed banana, milk, protein powder, melted oil, sugar, and leavening agents to the bowl and mix.
  - Spoon batter into a lined muffin tray.
  - Bake for 20-25 minutes.
  - Store in the fridge; toast before eating and top with peanut butter and banana slices.

notes: Vegan option available by using a chia egg (1 tbsp chia seeds + 3 tbsp water).
