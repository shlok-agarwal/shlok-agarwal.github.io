ingredients:
  - Serves 3
  - 1.5 cups dry rolled oats
  - 3 medium bananas
  - 6 tsp chia seeds
  - Cinnamon
  - 3 tbsp peanut butter

steps:
  - Divide oats, smashed banana, chia seeds, and cinnamon into 3 jars.
  - Add peanut butter (mixed in or on top).
  - Add 1/2 cup water to each jar and mix.
  - Soak overnight in the fridge.
