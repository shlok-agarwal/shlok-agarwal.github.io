ingredients:
  - Serves 4
  - 4 x 100g chicken breasts (skinless)
  - 4 medium sweet potatoes (peeled)
  - 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  - 1 onion
  - 1 tsp cooking oil
  - 4 tbsp pure tomato paste
  - Juice from 1 lemon
  - 2 tbsp fresh cilantro
  - 1-1.5 cups water
  - Spices (2 tsp curry powder, 1 tsp ginger, 1 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp garlic, 1/2 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp cinnamon, 1/4 tsp cloves)
  - Salt and pepper

steps:
  - Chop the onion and saut√© in a pot with oil until caramelized.
  - Chop the cherry tomatoes and add to the pot.
  - Add lemon juice, water, tomato paste, cilantro, and all spices to the pot and stir.
  - Slice chicken breast into pieces; peel and chop potatoes into quarters.
  - Add chicken and potatoes to the curry.
  - Simmer on the stove for 40-60 minutes covered with a lid, stirring occasionally.
  - Serve with cooked basmati rice.
