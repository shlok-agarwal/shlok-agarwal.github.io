ingredients:
  - Serves 3
  - 2/3 cup dry rolled oats
  - 2 medium ripe bananas
  - 4 large eggs (or chia eggs)
  - Toppings: 3 tbsp peanut butter, 2 cups fresh strawberries

steps:
  - Blend oats into a flour.
  - Add bananas and eggs to blender; blend until smooth.
  - Cook spoonfuls of batter in a non-stick pan until bubbling, then flip.
  - Cool and store in fridge.
  - To serve, heat in toaster and top with peanut butter and strawberries.

notes: Vegan option available using chia eggs.
