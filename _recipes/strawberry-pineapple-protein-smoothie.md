ingredients:
  - Serves 2
  - 2 cups frozen pineapple pieces
  - 4 cups frozen strawberries
  - 1.5 cups plain yogurt
  - 2 scoops vanilla protein powder
  - Water

steps:
  - Divide fruit and protein powder into 2 ziplock bags for freezing.
  - On the day, blend one bag with roughly 3/4 cup yogurt and water as needed.
