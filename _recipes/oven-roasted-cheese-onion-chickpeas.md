ingredients:
  - Serves 2
  - 1.5 cups cooked chickpeas
  - 2 tsp nutritional yeast
  - 1 tsp onion powder
  - Salt and pepper
  - Cooking oil spray

steps:
  - Preheat oven to 400F (200C).
  - Toss drained chickpeas on a baking tray with oil spray, nutritional yeast, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  - Roast for 30-40 minutes until crispy.

notes: Nutritional yeast gives the "cheesy" flavor.
