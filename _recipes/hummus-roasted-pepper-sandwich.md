ingredients:
  - Serves 3
  - 6 slices bread
  - 1 cup plain hummus
  - 300g red bell pepper
  - 6 cherry tomatoes
  - Lettuce mix
  - 3 tsp sunflower seeds

steps:
  - Preheat oven to 400F (200C).
  - Roast sliced bell peppers for 20-40 minutes.
  - Spread hummus on bread.
  - Top with lettuce, roasted peppers, tomato halves, and sunflower seeds.
