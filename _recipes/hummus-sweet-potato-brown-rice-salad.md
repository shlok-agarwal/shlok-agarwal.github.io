ingredients:
  - Serves 3
  - 3/4 cup plain hummus
  - 345g sweet potato
  - 1.5 cups cooked brown rice
  - 3 cups cherry tomatoes
  - 300g red bell pepper
  - 3 handfuls spinach
  - Dressing: 3 tsp olive oil, 3 tbsp vinegar, lemon juice

steps:
  - Preheat oven to 400F (200C).
  - Roast sliced sweet potato and bell peppers.
  - Cook brown rice.
  - Mix oil, vinegar, and lemon for dressing.
  - Assemble salad with spinach, rice, roasted veggies, tomatoes, and hummus.
  - Drizzle with dressing.
