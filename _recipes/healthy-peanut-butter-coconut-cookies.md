ingredients:
  - Serves 3 (Makes 12 cookies)
  - 1/4 cup peanut butter
  - 2 tsp chia seeds (+ 2 tbsp water)
  - 2 tbsp coconut sugar
  - 2 tbsp desiccated coconut
  - 1/2 tsp cinnamon

steps:
  - Preheat oven to 360F (180C).
  - Soak chia seeds in water for 5 minutes.
  - Mix peanut butter, sugar, coconut, cinnamon, and soaked chia seeds.
  - Form into small balls and flatten on a baking tray.
  - Bake for 10-15 minutes.
