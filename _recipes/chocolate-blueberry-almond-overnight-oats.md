ingredients:
  - Serves 2
  - 1 cup dry rolled oats
  - 4 tsp chia seeds
  - 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  - 2 tsp maple syrup
  - 1 cup frozen blueberries
  - 40 raw almonds
  - Cinnamon, water

steps:
  - Divide oats, chia seeds, almonds, cocoa powder, maple syrup, and cinnamon into 2 jars.
  - Add about 1/2 cup water to each and mix.
  - Add frozen blueberries and mix again.
  - Soak in the fridge for at least 8 hours.

notes: Can be eaten cold or heated briefly.
