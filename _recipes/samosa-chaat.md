description: A delicious street food combining crispy samosas with tangy chutneys and yogurt.

ingredients:
  - 4 medium-sized samosas (store-bought or homemade)
  - 1 cup plain yogurt (whisked smooth)
  - 1/2 cup tamarind chutney (imli chutney)
  - 1/4 cup green chutney (mint-coriander)
  - 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  - 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  - 1/4 cup sev (thin crispy noodles)
  - 2 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
  - 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  - 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  - 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  - Salt to taste

steps:
  - Break each samosa into 3-4 pieces and arrange them on a serving plate.
  - Add the boiled chickpeas around and on top of the samosa pieces.
  - Drizzle the whisked yogurt generously over the samosas.
  - Add the tamarind chutney in a zigzag pattern.
  - Drizzle the green chutney over the top.
  - Sprinkle the chopped onions evenly.
  - Add the chaat masala, roasted cumin powder, and red chili powder.
  - Top with sev and fresh coriander leaves.
  - Serve immediately while the samosas are still crispy.

photos:

    caption: Samosa Chaat ready to serve

notes: For the best taste, serve immediately after assembling. The contrast between crispy samosa and soft toppings is what makes this dish special. You can adjust the amount of chutneys based on your preference for sweetness and spice.
---

This is one of my favorite Indian street food recipes. The combination of crispy samosas with cool yogurt and tangy-sweet chutneys is absolutely delicious!
