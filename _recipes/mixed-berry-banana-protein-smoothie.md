ingredients:
  - Serves 2
  - 2 medium bananas
  - 2 cups mixed frozen berries
  - 1 small/medium avocado
  - 2 scoops vanilla protein powder
  - 4 tbsp dry rolled oats
  - 2 handfuls fresh baby spinach
  - 2 cups water (or as needed)

steps:
  - Peel and slice bananas and avocado.
  - Divide fruit, berries, protein powder, oats, and spinach into 2 ziplock bags for freezer prep.
  - On the day, blend the frozen mix with water until smooth.

notes: Avocado can be substituted with plain yogurt.
