ingredients:
  - Serves 2
  - 4 small chicken drumsticks
  - 1 cup cooked brown rice
  - 200g green beans
  - 200g bell peppers
  - 200g mushrooms
  - 1/2 onion
  - 2 tbsp coconut aminos/soy sauce
  - Spices: Cayenne, oregano, onion powder

steps:
  - Cook brown rice.
  - Season chicken with spices and coconut aminos; cook in a pan until browned and cooked through.
  - In a separate pan, saut√© onion, mushrooms, and peppers.
  - Add green beans and cook until tender.
  - Stir cooked rice and coconut aminos into the veggies.
  - Serve with chicken.
