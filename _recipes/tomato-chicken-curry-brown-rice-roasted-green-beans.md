ingredients:
  - Serves 3
  - 3 cups cooked brown rice
  - 360g chicken breast (skinless)
  - 3 cups cherry tomatoes
  - 300g green beans
  - 1/2 onion
  - 1-2 tsp curry powder
  - Onion powder, ginger powder, lemon juice, salt, pepper

steps:
  - Preheat oven to 400F (200C).
  - Roast green beans for 15-30 minutes.
  - Cook brown rice.
  - Saut√© onion in a pan.
  - Add chicken pieces, tomatoes, curry powder, and spices; simmer until cooked.
  - Serve curry with rice and roasted beans.
