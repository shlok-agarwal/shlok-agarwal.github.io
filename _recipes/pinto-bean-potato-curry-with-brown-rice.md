ingredients:

  - 390g potatoes
  - 1.5 cups cooked brown rice
  - 300g mushrooms
  - 300g green beans
  - 1/2 onion
  - 1 cup coconut milk
  - Spices: Curry powder, onion powder, ginger
  - Water

steps:
  - Cook brown rice.
  - Saut√© onion in a pot.
  - Add cubed potatoes and water; boil until soft.
  - Add mushrooms, green beans, pinto beans, coconut milk, and spices.
  - Simmer until veggies are cooked.
  - Serve with rice.
