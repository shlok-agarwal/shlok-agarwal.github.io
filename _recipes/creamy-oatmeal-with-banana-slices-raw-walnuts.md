ingredients:
  - Serves 1
  - 1/2 cup dry rolled oats
  - 1-2 cups water
  - 1 tsp coconut oil (flat)
  - 1 medium banana
  - 8 raw walnut halves
  - pinch cinnamon
  - 1 tsp xylitol (optional)

steps:
  - Bring the oats and water to a boil on the stove.
  - Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  - Stir in the cinnamon, coconut oil, and xylitol.
  - Serve with banana and walnuts.
