description: A refreshing and nutritious smoothie packed with berries and protein.
image: /recipes/assets/berry-smoothie.jpg

ingredients:
  - 1 cup frozen mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  - 1 ripe banana
  - 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  - 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  - 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  - 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  - 1 tbsp chia seeds
  - Ice cubes as needed

steps:
  - Add the almond milk and Greek yogurt to the blender first.
  - Add the frozen berries and banana.
  - Add the protein powder and chia seeds.
  - Blend on high for 60-90 seconds until smooth.
  - Taste and add honey if you prefer it sweeter.
  - Add ice cubes and blend again if you want it thicker.
  - Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.

notes: For meal prep, you can freeze the fruit portions in individual bags. Just add the liquid ingredients when ready to blend. The chia seeds add extra fiber and omega-3s.
---

This smoothie is my go-to breakfast when I'm short on time. It's filling, nutritious, and tastes amazing!
