ingredients:
  - Serves 3
  - 2/3 cup rolled oats
  - 1 medium ripe banana
  - 1/3 cup milk
  - 1 large egg (or chia egg)
  - 2 tsp coconut oil
  - 2 tsp coconut sugar
  - 1/4 cup vanilla protein powder
  - 3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  - 3/4 cup blueberries
  - 1/4 tsp cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder
  - Toppings: Peanut butter, raspberries

steps:
  - Preheat oven to 400F (200C).
  - Blend oats into flour.
  - Mash banana and mix with egg, milk, oil, and dry ingredients.
  - Fold in blueberries.
  - Bake in muffin tray for 20 minutes.
  - Toast before serving and top with peanut butter and raspberries.
