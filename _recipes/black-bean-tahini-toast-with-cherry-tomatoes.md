ingredients:
  - Serves ??
  - 1 cup cooked black beans
  - 4 slices bread
  - 2 tbsp tahini
  - 4 cherry tomatoes
  - small handful spinach/mixed lettuce
  - onion powder, oregano
  - lemon, salt and pepper

steps:
  - Roughly smash the cooked black beans
  - Add the tahini, lemon/lime juice, salt, pepper, oregano and onion powder to the black beans and mix to make a spread
  - Cut the cherry tomatoes in half.
  - Lightly toast the bread (optional) and add the black bean & tahini spread to each slice.
  - Top with a few lettuce leaves and cherry tomato halves
